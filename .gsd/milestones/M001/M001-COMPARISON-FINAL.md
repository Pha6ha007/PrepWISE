## Competitive Position Summary

### Where we WIN (unique advantages):
1. 🏆 **Voice AI Tutoring** — zero competitors
2. 🏆 **Long-term Memory** — zero competitors
3. 🏆 **Audio Explanations** — zero competitors
4. 🏆 **Study Journal with streaks** — unique for GMAT prep
5. 🏆 **Question of the Day** — unique interactive widget
6. ✅ **Question count #1** — 7,572 (overtook e-GMAT)
7. ✅ **100% GMAT Focus Edition** — some competitors still have AWA/SC
8. ✅ **Price** — $39-149/mo (below mid-tier, above budget)

### Where we LOSE (honest gaps):
1. 🔴 **Explanation coverage** — 46% vs 100% at competitors. 3,748 ReClor questions still without explanations
2. 🔴 **RAG depth** — 1,809 vectors vs estimated 10,000-50,000 at competitors. Sam's answers will be thinner on deep strategy
3. 🔴 **Brand/Trust** — 0 users, 0 reviews, 0 years. Competitors: 10-20 years, thousands of reviews
4. 🟡 **Video content** — 0 videos. TTP: 2,200, Magoosh: 200, e-GMAT: 250hrs. Voice is our substitute but some students want video
5. 🟡 **Official GMAC questions** — 0 vs Manhattan's 1,100. Can't use due to copyright
6. 🟡 **RC explanations** — 334 RC questions have 0 explanations

### Net Assessment:
**Продукт конкурентоспособен по features и quantity.** Слабее по quality depth (explanations, RAG).
Уникальные преимущества (Voice AI, Memory) дифференцируют от всех.
Готов к запуску — качество можно наращивать с revenue.
